Services

what we really know how

Event Management

Event management is the application of project management to the creation and development of large scale events such as festivals, conferences, ceremonies, formal parties, concerts, or conventions.

Full Technical Support

IWantEvents offer fully trained sound, lighting and stage technicians to run your event to a professional standard and quality.

Equipment Hire

Working with local hire companies IWantEvents can sub-hire equipment to your needs. IWE also has a small hire stock availible including sound, lighting and AV.

Schools DJ

IWantEvents provide a mobile DJ service to the local primary schools for different functions such as fundraisers to end of year parties.
